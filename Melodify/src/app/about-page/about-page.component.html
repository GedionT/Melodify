Melodify

The Melodify is a music generator that retrieves letters from the input website and translates it into pitches. It came out from a random idea, “how would it sound like if the website I am reading can play me a song?” A wikipedia page about Angular might sound like you are in a dungeon, your facebook page might sound so out of tune and silly. You have the option to choose different instruments, from piano to edm. Try it out, and hear what your favorite website tells you.
