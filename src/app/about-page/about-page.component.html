Melodify

Idea

The Melodify is a music generator that retrieves words from the a website and translates it into musical notes. It came out from a random idea, "What does a website sound like?‚Äù A wikipedia page about Angular might sound like you are in a dungeon, your facebook page might sound so out of tune and silly. You have the option to choose different instruments, from piano to edm. Try it out, and hear what your favorite website tells you.


How It Works

We first scrape a website of a portion of its words. Depending on the word a mucical note will be assigned - The first letter will dictate note, meanwhile the length will dictate duration. Using Keith William Horwood's dynamically generated synthetic keyboard we were able to map the words into a song.


Technologies

Angular.js, Bootstrap, CSS, HTML, JavaScript, Node.js, Puppeteer, and Typescript.
